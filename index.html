About Me

Engineer at the intersection of hardware, software, and autonomous systems.

I'm Jin Ziyu, a Robotics Systems undergraduate at the Singapore Institute of Technology. My engineering spans the full stack — from PCB design and embedded systems to ROS 2 navigation and web-based tooling.
